About Us

Gift Auto Industries, a unit of Adarsh Techno Corporation, has always desired to achieve good quality of nut bolts & tractor parts since we established our parent company in 1973.

We started with the manufacturing of tractor parts & Nut Bolts, and have always tried our best to manufacture and deliver the best quality products to our esteemed customers all over India.

Presently we are one of the leading manufacturers of nut bolts in India. Our clients have shown keen interest in our range as we provide them with innovative products at market leading prices.
